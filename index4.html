Shine Gardens is an agribuisiness venture located in Besease, Ghana involved in greenhouse production of vegetables, conventional farming, and poultry production We came into being based on the mutual passion of our co-founders who were at the time graduate students at Arizona state university looking for a way to make an impact back home in Ghana.Our business model allows us to operate as a social venture as we are committed to alongside making profits, training the next generation of Ghanaian Agripreneurs at no cost.Our vision is to emerge as a vertically integrated Agricultural enterprise serving the needs of the African continent and beyond, while continuing to equip furture agripeneurs with skills that allow them to suceesful start their own busineses
